# Equity Story

Equity story = why invest in this company, at this price, now.

## Route

- Unsettled moat/positioning: produce a diagnostic with competitive map, 2-3
  moat hypotheses, falsification signals, options, and verification actions.
- Settled thesis: produce the investor-facing equity story.

Ask only when stage, audience, valuation, legal context, or artifact matters.

## Must Do

Gather company/product, stage/geography, evidence, audience, ask/valuation, and
milestones. Mark figures `[fact]`, `[derived]`, `[estimate]`, `[assumption]`, or
`[open]`; never invent numbers. Use `startup-financial-modeling` for models.

Story spine: Why now, Why us, Why this, Why this price. Problem before
solution; market size separate from moat; proof next to claims. Research weak
claims; add objections, verification paths, landmines, bear case. Japan IPO
artifacts should be Japanese. Details: `build/references/playbook.md`;
examples: `build/references/examples.md`.

## Output Gate

Diagnostic gate: falsifiable moat hypotheses, honest options, verification
actions. Story order: Thesis; Why now; Problem; Insight & solution; Team;
Market; Business model; Competition & moat; Traction; Trajectory & ask; Vision;
Bear case; What would change our mind; Investor Q&A.

For decks, add heading/message slide outline; use slide-image skills for images.

Before finalizing, iterate until solid or blocked. Check stage fit, evidence,
bottom-up market logic, carry-home, no unsupported superlatives, verification
paths, metric definitions, landmines, and bear case.

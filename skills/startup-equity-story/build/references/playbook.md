# Equity Story Playbook

Use only when `SKILL.md` needs more detail. For worked patterns, also read
`examples.md`.

## Stage And Japan

Seed = team/insight/signal. Growth = retention, CAC/LTV, payback, NRR, margin,
scalable acquisition. IPO/IR = predictable growth, cash efficiency, governance,
risk disclosure, named comps. M&A = build-vs-buy, synergy, integration risk.

Japan work should be Japanese and map claims to 成長可能性資料, 想定問答集, and
business/risk text. Cover 引受審査 concerns: revenue quality, concentration,
related parties, IP, governance, runway, Growth-to-Prime, Japanese comps.

For 成長可能性資料, write the chain as 市場環境 -> 課題 -> サービス ->
KPI/収益モデル -> 競争優位 -> 成長戦略 -> リスク. For 想定問答, answer
valuation, churn, concentration, governance, use of proceeds, and post-listing
investment directly. For prospectus-style text, separate business description,
risks, and management issues; do not mix promotion with disclosure.

## Evidence And Objections

Research why-now inflections, market, substitutes, comps, regulation, customer
proof, unit economics. For thin claims, collect source/date/claim/why-it-matters
and leave unverified company-only facts as `[open]`. Use frameworks as proof
scaffolding: JTBD/beachhead for opportunity; 7 Powers, switching costs, network
effects, scale, or data for durability.

Handle objections by pairing each risk with evidence or an open item: market
too small, moat weak, team thin, valuation high, burn/runway, retention,
customer concentration, governance, and Japan IPO disclosure risk.

Prefer bottom-up TAM/SAM/SOM. Each load-bearing claim needs a verification path:
CRM, billing, cohort export, contract, filing, board material, patent, or
primary source.

## Craft

Begin with tension. Attach numbers to claims with comparison context. State the
contrarian core. Define over-readable words. Cut unsupported superlatives.
